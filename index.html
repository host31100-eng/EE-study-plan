Guide to Study Plan Colors

Hover over any course to highlight its relationships. Here is what the colors mean:

Hovered Course
The course currently being viewed.
i
Course Resources
Select the 'info' (i) icon to access additional course materials.
Prerequisite Chain (Gradient Sequence)
This gradient displays the sequence of prerequisites.
Deep Red (Solid): Direct Prerequisite. Mandatory course that must be completed prior to enrollment.
Fading / Dashed Red: Indirect Prerequisite. Foundational course further back in the sequence. Gradient hues (e.g., lighter colors, or different border patterns) indicate distance in the chain.
Unlocks (Post-requisite)
Courses that may be taken after successful completion.
Co-requisite Course
A course that must be taken concurrently.
